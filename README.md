## What is AEO?

**Answer Engine Optimization (AEO)** is the practice of ensuring your brand gets mentioned — and mentioned favorably — when AI assistants answer shopping queries. As more customers ask AI "what should I buy?", your shelf space is no longer physical or even digital — it's the AI's response.

**Pixii AEO Scout** scans multiple LLMs (Gemini, Llama) and Amazon Best Seller data simultaneously. It extracts brand mentions, detects market dominance or AI blind spots, and produces a combined A–F visibility grade card.

## Features

- **Dual-Engine AI Scanning** — Llama (Groq) and Gemini (Google) queried in parallel.
- **Amazon Cross-Referencing** — Compares AI results directly against Amazon Best Seller Ranks (BSR).
- **Market Dominance Detection** — Automatically identifies clear market leaders and hidden "AI Blind Spots".
- **Combined Score Card** — Calculates an A–F visibility grade based on presence across all engines.
- **High-Density Reporting** — Premium, dark-mode analytics dashboard.
